Save your favorite Websites

Savite was created to help you to save your favorite websites, categorize them, and give yourself time to read later on.

Just a few steps


Save links

Add a URL to your favorite website.

Screen shot

Savite will automatically save a screen shot of the provided URL. Helping you visualize remember why certain website was important.

Categorize

Pick a category for your website.

Deadline

Pick a deadline to read. Savite will not show you webistes after the deadline expired. Savite will keep it in case you change your mind later on.
