Save your favorite Websites

Savite was created to help you to save your favorite websites, categorize them, and give yourself time to read them later on.

Just a few steps


Save links

Add a URL to your favorite website.

Screen shot

Savite will automatically save a screen shot of the URL helping you to visually remember why a certain content was important.

Categorize

Pick a category for your website.

Deadline

Give yourself a deadline to read it by. Savite will not show you the content after the deadline expires.
